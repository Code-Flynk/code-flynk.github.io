Bio | Intro

Welcome to my portfolio page that shares my work from my github Code-Flynk portfolio. I am a professional mechanical engineer who is pursuing a path in coding. My work resembles a variety of projects that illustrate both my journey on this path as well as my skills in illustrating design criteria and constraints through coding. I can apply my extensive design experience to coding in an effort to build a relationship between the virtual and physical aspects of engineering applications.

Pacman Exercise: This is an adaptation of classic Pacman to illustrate movement and visual correspondence exercise for MIT Frontend Development course. I wanted to make it unique by adding a pulsing eye as Pacman moves and having a unique orange tint.
